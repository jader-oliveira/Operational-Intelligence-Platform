# AI Safety and Trust

## Safety controls

- Structured output schemas.
- Evidence-required claims.
- Challenge agent.
- Deterministic policy checks.
- Permission-aware retrieval and tools.
- Human approval.
- Change risk classification.
- Recommendation expiration.
- Dry-run and post-verification.
- Kill switch for model or automation capability.

## AI trust score

The trust score is not a single subjective number. It is composed from:

- Evidence coverage.
- Citation validity.
- Historical task accuracy.
- Confidence calibration.
- Contradiction severity.
- Data freshness.
- Model and prompt version status.
- Policy compliance.
- Similar-case success rate.

The UI must expose the components, not only the aggregate score.
