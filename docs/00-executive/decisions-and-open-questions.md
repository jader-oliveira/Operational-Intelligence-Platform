# Decisions and Open Questions

## Decisions already made

- Advisory-first; no autonomous production changes in early releases.
- Human approval is mandatory for execution.
- The platform is multi-model and vendor-neutral.
- Deterministic policies are separate from model reasoning.
- Evidence, provenance, confidence, and contradictory evidence are first-class data.
- AI performance and business value are measured continuously.
- Operations DNA and a decision graph are core differentiators.

## Open questions to resolve during Phase 0

- Exact Xen platform and API versions in scope.
- Existing monitoring, logging, ITSM, CMDB, backup, and identity systems to integrate.
- Data residency and retention rules.
- GPU infrastructure available for inference and fine-tuning.
- Which workloads permit snapshots and under what consistency rules.
- Required regulatory or contractual frameworks.
- Pilot business service and success baseline.
- Preferred graph technology after a measured proof of concept.
