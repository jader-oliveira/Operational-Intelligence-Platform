# Team and Responsibilities

## Minimum core team

- Product owner or domain lead.
- Principal platform architect.
- Two backend/platform engineers.
- Connector engineer.
- Data engineer.
- ML engineer.
- SRE or observability engineer.
- Security engineer.
- Frontend engineer.
- QA/automation engineer.
- Part-time VMware and Xen subject-matter experts.

## Governance forums

- Weekly architecture review.
- Weekly delivery review.
- Biweekly AI evaluation review.
- Monthly security and risk review.
- Pilot service-owner review.
