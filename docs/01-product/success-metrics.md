# Success Metrics

## Operational outcomes

- Mean time to detect.
- Mean time to acknowledge.
- Mean time to identify likely root cause.
- Mean time to resolve.
- Repeat-incident rate.
- Change failure rate.
- Capacity incidents avoided.
- Security findings remediated.

## AI effectiveness

- Incidents detected by AI.
- True positive, false positive, and missed-incident rates.
- Root-cause top-1 and top-3 accuracy.
- Recommendation acceptance rate.
- Recommendation correctness after execution.
- Recommendation rejection reasons.
- Automation success and rollback rates.
- Percentage of answers with sufficient evidence.
- Confidence calibration error.
- Challenge-agent contradiction rate.
- Hallucination or unsupported-claim rate.
- Model latency, token usage, GPU time, and cost.

## Business value

- Engineer hours saved.
- Downtime avoided.
- Recurring incident reduction.
- Audit preparation effort reduced.
- Capacity expenditure deferred or better planned.
- Percentage of estate covered.
