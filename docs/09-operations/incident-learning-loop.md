# Incident Learning Loop

1. Resolve incident.
2. Verify technical and business recovery.
3. Capture confirmed root cause and contributing factors.
4. Record evidence that was useful or misleading.
5. Score recommendations and actions.
6. Update runbooks, graph, policies, and ownership.
7. Add reviewed case to evaluation suite.
8. Promote to training dataset only after quality and privacy approval.
9. Re-run relevant models and compare.
10. Track whether the incident recurs.
